1. What Information We Collect

Location Information. TimestampCam is a watermark camera application. When you grant location permission, the App accesses your device's GPS coordinates and converts them into a human-readable address (via on-device reverse geocoding) to embed as a visible watermark on your photos and videos. Location data is processed entirely on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third party. The location watermark is permanently embedded into the image or video file at the moment of capture. Granting location permission is entirely optional — you may use the App's camera and watermark features without enabling location access, and the location element will simply be omitted from the watermark.

Camera and Microphone Data. The App accesses your device camera and microphone solely to capture photos and videos as directed by you. Captured media is saved directly to your device storage. We do not transmit your photos or videos to our servers.

Device and Log Information. To ensure the App functions properly, improve performance, and maintain security, we may collect device and log information including: unique device identifier, device model, operating system version, system language, country, IP address, software version number, network type, and service log information.

Feedback Information. When you contact us for support or submit feedback, we collect the information you provide (such as your email address and the content of your message) in order to respond to you.

2. Which Permissions We May Request

TimestampCam requests only the permissions necessary to deliver its core features. All permissions are requested following the principle of minimal data collection, and you may grant or revoke them at any time in your device's system settings.

Camera — Required to capture photos and videos. Without this permission the App cannot function.

Microphone — Required to record audio when capturing video. You may deny this permission if you do not wish to record audio; video can still be captured without sound.

Precise Location (ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION) — Optional. TimestampCam may request access to your device's precise GPS location solely to embed location information (GPS coordinates and/or address) as a visible watermark on your photos and videos. This feature supports documentation and proof-of-location use cases such as construction records, property inspections, and delivery confirmation. Location data is:
  • Accessed only while the App is in active use (foreground only);
  • Processed entirely on your device — it is never uploaded to our servers;
  • Embedded permanently into the photo or video file at the time of capture;
  • Never used for advertising, tracking, or profiling purposes.
If you deny location permission, all other App features remain fully available; the location watermark element will not appear.

Storage / Media Access — Required to save captured photos and videos to your device gallery, and to import existing media for watermarking. On Android 10 and above, the App uses the system MediaStore API and does not require broad storage access.

Internet Access — Used to synchronize time with NTP (Network Time Protocol) servers to ensure the timestamp watermark reflects verified, accurate time. Also used for optional reverse geocoding (converting GPS coordinates to a readable address) and for crash reporting. The App functions in offline mode with reduced features when no network is available.

You can check or change the status of any permission at any time in your device's system settings. Revoking a permission will disable the corresponding feature but will not affect previously captured media or previously granted permissions.
